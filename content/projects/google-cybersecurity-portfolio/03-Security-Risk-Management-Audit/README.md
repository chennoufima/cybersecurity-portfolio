# Security Audit & Risk Management: Botium Toys

## Scenario
In this project, I conducted an internal security audit for "Botium Toys," a fictional retail company. The goal was to evaluate their current security posture, identify gaps in compliance (PCI DSS and GDPR), and provide risk-based recommendations to the management team.

## Objectives
- Conduct a **Controls Assessment** to identify missing technical, operational, and physical safeguards.
- Perform a **Compliance Audit** against PCI DSS (for credit card data) and GDPR (for user privacy).
- Apply the **NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)** to prioritize threats.
- Draft professional security recommendations for stakeholders.

## Tools & Frameworks
- **NIST RMF** (Categorize, Select, Implement, Assess, Authorize, Monitor)
- **Compliance Standards:** PCI DSS, GDPR, SOC1/SOC2
- **Risk Assessment Matrix**
