As part of my transition into cybersecurity, I designed and implemented a secure network environment to simulate enterprise-level threat detection and prevention.

### Key Features:
- **Intrusion Detection/Prevention:** Deployed **Suricata** on the pfSense WAN interface to monitor, log, and automatically block malicious traffic patterns.
- **Traffic Analysis:** Configured custom rule sets to identify simulated SQL injection and Brute Force attacks.
- **Secure Architecture:** Implemented VLAN segmentation to isolate vulnerable services from the management network, adhering to the principle of **Least Privilege**.
- **Logging & Auditing:** Utilized pfSense system logs and Suricata alerts to perform post-incident analysis and refine firewall policies.
